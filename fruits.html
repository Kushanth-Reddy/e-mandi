What are the benefits of eating fruits daily?

A healthy diet consists of fresh fruits that are packed with antioxidants, vitamins, minerals & a wide range of powerful plant compounds. Fruits provide a wide range of nutrients at relatively lower calories which is why they’re considered to be one of the healthiest sources of nutrition. Regular consumption of fruits is known to boost your immunity, lower blood pressure, ensures a healthy heart & aids in digestion as well. This is why it’s necessary to include fresh fruits in your daily diet.
